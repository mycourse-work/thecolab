# Welcome to the Narkcoin Kingdom

Somewhere between "let's build a friendly AI moderation bot" and "let's give it a sense of humour," TheColab.ai's Watercooler channel ended up with its own fictional currency, its own gambling casino, its own appeals court, and a nightly public execution of its richest members. This course is your field guide to all of it.

> **Important:** Narkcoin has no real-world value and cannot be cashed out. Everything in this course describes an in-joke satirical economy that only operates inside Watercooler — treat the mechanics seriously enough to understand them properly, and not one bit more seriously than that.

## What you'll learn

- **Module 1 — Origins of the Narkcoin Kingdom**: how a throwaway joke turned into a full economy, and who's running it — The Colab Clawd (Treasury and referee) and Karen (Chief Complaints Officer).
- **Module 2 — How Nark Credits Work**: the passive income of message karma and reactions, and the high-stakes art of filing (and appealing) a nark.
- **Module 3 — Gambling, Games & the Midnight Guillotine**: the full Watercooler casino, and the nightly wealth-reset event that keeps any single member from running away with the leaderboard.

By the end, you'll understand the whole system well enough to read a `/narkledger` output and know exactly what happened — and why.
