# Welcome to AI For Small Business

You've probably heard a lot about AI lately — from the news, from your accountant, maybe even from your competitors. It can feel overwhelming, but here's the good news: **you don't need to be a tech expert to use AI in your business.**

This course is built for everyday Kiwi business owners and operators. Whether you're running a café in Ponsonby, a building company in Christchurch, a farm in the Waikato, or a consultancy in Wellington — there are practical, affordable AI tools that can save you time, money, and headaches.

## What You'll Learn

By the end of this course, you'll be able to:

- **Understand** what AI actually is (and isn't) — without the jargon
- **Assess** whether your business is ready for AI and where to start
- **Choose** the right AI tools for your specific needs and budget
- **Use** AI for everyday tasks like marketing, admin, and finances
- **Stay legal** with NZ privacy law, the Fair Trading Act, and your responsibilities
- **Implement** AI step-by-step without wasting money on the wrong things
- **Set up** simple governance so your team uses AI safely
- **Plan ahead** for where AI is going and what it means for your business

## Who Is This For?

This course is designed for:

- Small business owners and operators (1-50 employees)
- Sole traders and freelancers
- Managers and team leaders in small organisations
- Anyone curious about using AI to work smarter

**No technical skills required.** If you can use email and a smartphone, you can do this course.

## How It Works

The course has **8 modules**, each with 5 short lessons and a quiz. You can work through them in order, or jump to the topics that matter most to you right now. Most people complete the full course in 4-6 hours, but there's no rush — go at your own pace.

## The NZ Context

This isn't a generic "AI for business" course from overseas. Everything here is grounded in the New Zealand context:

- NZ case studies from businesses like yours
- NZ-specific tools (Xero, MYOB, Hnry, Tradify, and more)
- NZ law (Privacy Act, Fair Trading Act, Te Tiriti obligations)
- NZ government support and funding you can actually access
- Real stats from the AI Forum of New Zealand
