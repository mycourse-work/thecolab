# NZ Business Tools with AI Built In

Here's something most NZ business owners don't realise: the software you're already paying for probably has AI features you're not using. Xero, MYOB, Employment Hero, and a bunch of other Kiwi business tools have been quietly adding AI capabilities — and many of them are included in your existing subscription.

Before you go shopping for new AI tools, let's make sure you're getting the most out of what you've got.

> **What you'll learn:** Identify AI features already built into tools you pay for (Xero, MYOB, Employment Hero) | Activate hidden AI capabilities in your existing accounting and HR software | Evaluate NZ-built industry-specific tools for trades, farming, and retail | Determine whether you need to buy new AI tools or just switch on what you have

## Accounting and Finance

### Xero — JAX AI

Xero's AI assistant JAX is a game-changer for small business accounting. If you're one of the hundreds of thousands of NZ businesses already on Xero, these features are included in your plan.

**What JAX does:**
- **Auto-categorises expenses** — learns from your past transactions and categorises new ones automatically
- **Bank reconciliation** — matches transactions and suggests coding with increasing accuracy over time
- **Payment prediction** — tells you when invoices are likely to be paid based on customer history
- **Cash flow forecasting** — projects your cash position forward so you can plan ahead

**Pricing:** From ~NZ$33/month. AI features are included — no extra charge.

> **Tip:** If you're still manually reconciling bank transactions in Xero, turn on auto-categorisation. It learns from your corrections, so the more you use it, the smarter it gets.

### MYOB

If you're on MYOB instead of Xero, you've got AI features too:
- **Smart Invoice Reminders** — chases up late invoices automatically
- **AI Business Insights** — surfaces trends and anomalies in your financials
- **AI BAS Prep** — for businesses with Australian operations
- **AI Coding Suggestions** — auto-codes transactions similar to Xero

### Hnry — Tax on Autopilot for Sole Traders

If you're a sole trader, contractor, or freelancer, Hnry is worth knowing about. It automatically calculates and pays your income tax, GST, ACC, and student loan with every payment you receive.

**Pricing:** 1% of your self-employed income, capped at $1,500/year. That's it.

**Who it's for:** Sole traders, contractors, and freelancers who'd rather spend time on their business than on tax returns.

### Dext — Receipt and Invoice Processing

Dext uses AI to read your receipts and invoices, extract the data, and push it straight into Xero or MYOB. Snap a photo of a receipt, and it's done.

### Figured — Farm Accounting

If you're in agriculture, Figured integrates with Xero to provide farm-specific financial management — livestock tracking, feed budgets, and compliance reporting with AI-assisted insights.

## HR and Employment

### Employment Hero

Employment Hero describes itself as NZ's first AI Employment Operating System, and it's a big deal for small businesses drowning in HR admin. It covers payroll, HR, benefits, and recruitment in one platform, with AI reducing admin time by 30–40%.

**What the AI does:**
- Generates compliant employment agreements
- Automates leave management and approvals
- Streamlines recruitment with AI-assisted screening
- Handles payroll calculations and compliance

**Who it's for:** Any business with employees that wants to spend less time on HR paperwork.

### Deel AI Workforce

Deel offers pre-built AI agents across HR, payroll, and talent management. It's particularly useful if you have contractors or remote workers, including overseas team members.

## How to Find Hidden AI Features in Your Existing Tools

Most software providers have been adding AI features without making a big fuss about it. Here's how to find what you're missing:

1. **Check your software provider's blog or release notes** — search for "AI" or "automation" in recent updates
2. **Look in your settings** — many AI features need to be toggled on
3. **Ask your accountant or bookkeeper** — they'll know about Xero/MYOB AI features you're not using
4. **Contact support** — ask specifically "What AI or automation features does my plan include?"

You might be surprised. Many businesses are paying for AI features they've never switched on.

## Before You Buy Anything New

Before spending money on new AI tools, work through this checklist:

- Have you turned on all the AI features in your existing accounting software?
- Is your HR platform's automation fully configured?
- Are you using the scheduling and quoting features in your industry tool?
- Have you checked whether your email/office platform includes AI (Google Workspace, Microsoft 365)?

The AI Forum of NZ reports that 75% of organisations found their AI setup costs were under $5,000. For many businesses, the cost is closer to zero — because the tools are already paid for.

## Key Takeaways

- Check your existing software first — Xero, MYOB, and other tools you already pay for likely have AI features you're not using
- Xero's JAX AI handles auto-categorisation, reconciliation, and cash flow forecasting at no extra cost
- Hnry automates tax for sole traders at just 1% of income (capped at $1,500/year)
- NZ-built tools like Tradify, Fergus, Timely, and Figured understand Kiwi business requirements
