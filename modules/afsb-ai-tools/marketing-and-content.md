# Marketing and Content Creation

Marketing is one of the areas where AI delivers the fastest, most visible results for small businesses. Whether you need social media posts, blog articles, product descriptions, or professional graphics, AI tools can cut the time from hours to minutes.

But there's a catch — and we'll get to that. First, let's look at what's available.

> **What you'll learn:** Select the right AI tools for design, social media, copywriting, and video creation | Apply a practical content workflow (brief-review-edit-publish) to cut creation time by 75% | Maintain your brand voice when using AI-generated marketing content | Recognise your Fair Trading Act responsibilities for AI-generated claims

## Design and Visual Content

### Canva AI (Magic Studio)

If you're not already using Canva, you're probably the exception. Canva's Magic Studio takes things further with AI-powered features that make professional design genuinely accessible.

**What Magic Studio does:**
- **Magic Design** — describe what you want and it generates complete designs
- **Magic Write** — AI copywriting directly inside your designs
- **Magic Eraser** — remove unwanted objects from photos
- **Background Remover** — clean product photography in one click
- **Brand Kit** — keeps your colours, fonts, and logos consistent across everything

| Plan | Price | What You Get |
|---|---|---|
| Free | $0 | Basic features, limited AI uses |
| Pro | $15/month | Full Magic Studio, 1TB storage, Brand Kit |
| Business | $20/person/month | Team features, brand controls, unlimited folders |

**Who it's for:** Every small business. Seriously. Whether you're creating social media posts, flyers, presentations, or product images, Canva Pro with Magic Studio is one of the best-value AI subscriptions you can get.

> **Tip:** Start with Canva's free tier, but don't ignore the Pro trial. The Brand Kit feature alone — which locks in your colours, fonts, and logo so every design looks consistent — is worth the $15/month.

### HeyGen — AI Video Without a Camera

HeyGen creates professional-looking videos using AI avatars. You type a script, choose an avatar, and it generates a video with realistic lip-syncing and natural speech.

**Pricing:** From $24/month.

**Practical uses:**
- Product explainer videos
- Welcome messages for your website
- Training videos for staff
- Social media video content

> **Warning:** AI-generated video is getting better fast, but it's not perfect. Always preview your videos before publishing — odd lip movements or unnatural pauses can undermine your credibility.

## Social Media Management

### Buffer

Buffer helps you schedule social media posts across multiple platforms. Its AI assistant can suggest post ideas, rewrite content for different platforms, and recommend the best times to post.

| Plan | Price | What You Get |
|---|---|---|
| Free | $0 | 3 social channels, basic scheduling |
| Essentials | $5–6/channel/month | Analytics, engagement tools, AI assistant |
| Team | $10/channel/month | Collaboration, approval workflows |

**Who it's for:** Any business posting to more than one social media platform. The free tier covers three channels, which is enough for most small businesses starting out.

## AI-Powered Copywriting

### Jasper AI

Jasper is purpose-built for marketing copy. It generates blog posts, ad copy, email campaigns, social media content, and SEO-optimised articles.

**Pricing:** From $39/month per seat.

**What makes it different from ChatGPT/Claude:** Jasper is specifically trained for marketing. It understands marketing frameworks, can maintain brand voice across campaigns, and integrates with tools like Surfer SEO for search optimisation.

**Who it's for:** Businesses producing a lot of marketing content — blogs, newsletters, ad campaigns. If you only need occasional help with writing, ChatGPT or Claude will do the job for less money.

### Using AI Assistants for Content

You don't need a specialised tool for most marketing writing. ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini can handle:

- **Blog posts** — give it a topic, target audience, and word count
- **Newsletters** — paste in your key points and let it draft the body
- **Product descriptions** — feed it features and it'll write compelling copy
- **Email sequences** — outline your campaign and it'll draft each email
- **Social media posts** — ask for variations across platforms

## A Practical Content Workflow

Here's a workflow that works for most small businesses:

1. **Brief the AI** — "Write a social media post for our plumbing business about our 24/7 emergency call-out service. Tone: friendly and professional. Include a call to action."
2. **Review the output** — Read it carefully. Does it sound like you? Is everything accurate?
3. **Edit and personalise** — Add local details, your own voice, specific offers. This is the step most people skip, and it makes all the difference.
4. **Check for accuracy** — AI can make things up. Double-check any claims, statistics, or specific details.
5. **Publish** — Schedule it through Buffer or post directly.

This takes about 10 minutes compared to the 45-60 minutes it might take to write from scratch. Over a week of daily posts, that's hours saved.

## Maintaining Your Brand Voice

One of the biggest risks with AI-generated content is that everything starts sounding the same — generic, polished, and a bit lifeless. Here's how to keep your brand voice intact:

- **Create a brand voice document** — describe your tone, words you use and avoid, and examples of content that sounds like you. Feed this to the AI as context.
- **Always edit AI output** — treat AI content as a first draft, never a finished product
- **Add personal stories and local references** — AI can't know about your experience at the Fieldays or your regular at the local cafe
- **Mix AI and human content** — not every post needs AI. Sometimes a quick photo and a genuine caption beats a polished AI-generated post

> **Important:** Under the Fair Trading Act, you can't make misleading claims — and that includes claims generated by AI. If the AI writes "we're the best plumber in Auckland," you'd better be able to back that up. Always check AI-generated marketing copy for accuracy and compliance.
